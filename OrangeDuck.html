Orange Duck


  • Used to live in a lake filled with orange juice
  • Can fly and swim
  • Quacks

The orange duck is an illusive, and rare type of duck found only in the northern parts of Florida. They were accidently created in 1962 when a local factory spilled 250 thousand gallons of orange juice into local ponds. Since then they have been found all over the southern coast of the United States. Their primary food source is gummy worms and they are offten preyed upon by parched hunters.
